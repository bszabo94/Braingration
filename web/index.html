Braingration

This interactive map is based upon the data of Wikipedia. The dataset had been fetched using SPARQL queries againts the endpoint of DBPedia, which is a structured form of data represented in Wikipedia.

The map aims to provide an insight of the migration of intellectuals over human history.

Countries can be selected to reveal both immigration and emigration of sceintists of the particular country, as well as focal points of both birthplaces and travel destinations.
